new Sprint(projectID, name, beginDate, endDate)

Construct a new sprint linkedUserStories {Array[String]} : Contains the issue linked to this sprint totalDifficulty {Int} : Sum of the difficulty of the issues linked to this sprint state {String} : The state of the sprint ('toDo', 'onGoing' or 'end') color {String} : color for the div containing the display of the sprint on the differents pages
Parameters:

beginDate String The date of the begining of the sprint
endDate String The date of the ending of the sprint
